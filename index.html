Villa Grandinetti

July 25th

Villa Grandinetti is hosting the wedding ceremony (on the beach) and reception. It is a beautiful villa with a pool and a view of the sea. The villa is located in the town of Falerna Scalo, which is about 30 minutes from Tropea and about 10 minutes from the Lamezia Terme airport.

Falerno Scalo

July 24th - 26th

Falerna Scalo is a small local beach town (not far from Amantea) located 4 minutes to Villa Grandinetti and 18 minutes from the airport by car / taxi. We are suggesting all guests to have reservations to sleep in this town the night of the wedding so that getting home after the party is simple. The night before the wedding guests can also enjoy Riva restaurant and lounge bar.

Tropea

July 26th+

“Most beautiful village in Italy” for 2021, Tropea is a seaside resort with sandy beaches. Legends suggests that the town was founded by Hercules himself.

We suggest reserving a hotel walking distance to the historical city center. This center is full of action and amazing restaurants (Vecchio Forno is one of our favorites). For even more adventure, you can travel south along the coast of Capo Vaticano for amazing cliff top views. Also, an amazing excursion from Tropea is the boat trip to visit the Aeolian Islands. View this guide 7 things to do in Tropea to discover more!
