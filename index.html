Villa Grandinetti

Day of wedding

The wedding ceremony and reception will take place at Villa Grandinetti. It is a beautiful villa with a pool and garden next to the sea. The villa is located in the town of Falerna Scalo, which is about 50 minutes from Tropea and about 10 minutes from the Lamezia Terme airport.

Falerno Scalo

Pre Wedding

Falerna Scalo is a small local beach town located 5 minutes from Villa Grandinetti and 15 minutes from the airport by car or taxi. We are suggesting all guests to have reservations to sleep in this town the night of the wedding so that getting home after the party is simple.

Here are some hotel suggestions, if they are unavailable contact them on WhatsApp:

  • CoolBay Beach & Pool
  • Hotel Paradiso
  • Crialesi Boutique Rooms
  • Lions Hotel
  • Cidda Hotel

Tropea

Post Wedding

“Most beautiful village in Italy” for 2021, Tropea is a seaside resort with sandy beaches. This historical city center is full of action and amazing restaurants. View this guide 7 things to do in Tropea to discover more!

We suggest reserving a hotel walking distance to the city center:

  • Aether Suites (Free Parking)
  • B&B Chez Moi
  • Magnoli'Apartment
  • Tropea Boutique Hotel
